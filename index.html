How to Vote in India

6 steps every voter should know

1

Check Your Voter Registration

Visit voterportal.eci.gov.in or the Voter Helpline App. Search by EPIC number or name and address. Not registered? Fill Form 6 online. Registration closes 7 days before the notification date.

2

Know Your Constituency

India has 543 Lok Sabha constituencies, each electing one MP. Find yours on the ECI portal using your address. Some seats are reserved for SC or ST candidates only.

3

Get Your EPIC Card

EPIC (Elector's Photo Identity Card) is your official voter ID from ECI. Apply at voterportal.eci.gov.in or at your local BLO office. Lost it? You can still vote with any ONE of 12 approved documents: Aadhaar, PAN, Passport, Driving Licence, MNREGA Job Card, Bank Passbook with photo, Health Insurance Smart Card, Pension document with photo, NPR Smart Card, Service ID, Official photo ID by PSU, or Smart Card by RGI.

4

Find Your Polling Booth

Your booth address is on your voter slip (delivered before election day). Also findable on ECI portal using your EPIC number. Booths are typically within 2 km of your address. Voter slip is NOT mandatory — your name on the roll is sufficient.

5

Cast Your Vote on the EVM

Show your ID to the polling officer. They mark the register and apply ink to your left index finger. Press the blue button next to your candidate on the EVM. It beeps once. The VVPAT shows a paper slip for 7 seconds.

6

Understand NOTA

NOTA (None of the Above) is the last option on every EVM (button 99AA). Introduced by Supreme Court order in 2013. Choosing NOTA means you reject all candidates. Even if NOTA gets the most votes, the candidate with the next highest votes still wins.

How to use this simulator:

  1. Control Unit: Click the "BALLOT" button. This simulates the Polling Officer enabling the machine for you.
  2. Balloting Unit: The "READY" light will turn green. Press the blue button next to your chosen candidate.
  3. Confirmation: You will hear a long beep. The "BUSY" light will turn red.
  4. VVPAT: Look at the VVPAT window. Your selected candidate's slip will appear for 7 seconds before dropping.
